My Future Plans

#6. My Plans :)

Through this journey, I learned that I like creating things. I do not like repetive office jobs, I like to connect with people, and I like working on important tasks even if they are difficult by nature. This is why I do not want to go into an easy career path.

I want to go into Mechatronics Engineering at the University of Waterloo or the University of Toronto. Mechatronics is a field of engineering where you cover a lot of different strands of engineering. It is not specific like Mechanical Engineering or Electrical Engineering, but it's benefit is that I get a taste of the different fields of engineering. Along with this, I have less theory to learn in depth, instead it would be more generalized to cover more content.

The Last Push

I feel Mechatronics Engineering will be really beneficial into getting to a variety of jobs as an Engineer. I also would like to get a business degree eventually as I would need one to go into project management which involves taking leadership over a group of hardworking people. Project Management links to both of my skills: creation and interpersonal skills. The path of my career will change as I progress through life as the first job you have is not the one you are stuck with your whole career. I saw this with my Co-Op experience as well, I will constantly improve and iterate until I find what I am passionate about.

Regardless of what my future holds, I am sure I will evolve as a person and ultimately succeed with what I want to do in life. I believe the best way to think right now is not to define my whole life ahead of me, instead I should work on the skills I want to build to have the most successful future. Right now, I need to make sure my grades are high and that I am gaining as much work experience as possible as a student.

This journey has been absolutely incredible, and I am glad you took the time to read it from start to finish. Please free to contact me at malhotrakeshav10@gmail.com to keep in touch. Thank you!
